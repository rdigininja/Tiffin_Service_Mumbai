Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

Is the food hygienic?

+

Our kitchens are FSSAI-certified! We follow strict hygiene protocols with daily kitchen deep cleaning, chef health checks, and quality-controlled ingredients. All tiffins are packed in food-grade containers.

Can I pause my plan?

+

Yes! Just inform us 1 day prior. You can pause for vacations or when you have excess food at home. We'll adjust your billing accordingly.

What are your delivery timings?

+

Lunch is delivered between 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM and dinner between 7:00 PM - 8:00 PM. We provide 30-minute delivery windows when you subscribe.

How do payments work?

+

We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, and cash on delivery. Monthly plans require advance payment. Daily plans can be paid weekly.

Do you offer diet-specific meals?

+

Yes! We provide Jain, Keto, Diabetic-friendly, and Low-calorie options. Mention your requirements when ordering.
